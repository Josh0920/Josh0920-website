For Me to Live Is Christ

Joshua M. Haub · July 6, 2024

In the words of a dear friend of mine, speaking on Philippians 1:21:

“It’s only when you can say, ‘for me to live is Christ’ that you can say ‘to die is gain’”

What do we live for? If we live for something other than Christ, death is the enemy of my “dream” life.

Paul lived out Mark 8:34–38 in Philippians 1. Paul’s life was totally FOR CHRIST. Yet, too often we are like the Philippians, living for ourselves, only caring about ourselves, and needing to be reminded to “live in harmony in the Lord” with one another (Phil 4:2). Let us live out Phil 2:1–5, having the attitude of Christ. If Christ was willing to give up being in Heaven to wash our feet, we need to be willing to put each other first. Are we really better than God?

To Him be the Glory.

Joshua M. Haub · July 6, 2024
