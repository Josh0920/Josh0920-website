Matthew 8–9 — Do You Believe?

Joshua M. Haub · December 7, 2024

As you read through Matthew 8–9, you pick up on some key principles:

  1. Jesus is willing to heal (8:2–3)
  2. Jesus positively looks at great faith (8:5–10, 13)
  3. Jesus calms the storms of life (8:23–27)
  4. Jesus is God’s Son (8:28–29)
  5. Jesus costs people things (8:33–34)
  6. Jesus, the One desperately needed (9:2)
  7. Jesus can heal and forgive sins (9:3–8)
  8. Jesus can make dead people live (9:18)
  9. Jesus is the source of healing (9:20–22)
  10. Jesus can raise from the dead (9:24)

Then…

“As Jesus went on from there, two blind men followed Him, crying out, “Have mercy on us, Son of David!” When He entered the house, the blind men came up to Him, and Jesus said to them, “Do you believe that I am able to do this?” They said to Him, “Yes, Lord.” Then He touched their eyes, saying, “It shall be done to you according to your faith.”” (Matthew 9:27–29, NASB95)

Do we believe these things? Some did; some didn’t. What about you?

To Him be the Glory.
