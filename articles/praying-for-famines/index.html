Praying for Famines

Joshua M. Haub · February 7, 2025

“Now when he had spent everything, a severe famine occurred in that country, and he began to be impoverished.” (Luke 15:14, NASB95)

We need to pray for God to send famines (hard times) into peoples’ lives, not because we love seeing people suffer, but because we want people to turn back to the Lord.

Would we rather people have a nice life or be saved?

Think about the Prodigal Son.

To Him be the Glory.

Joshua M. Haub · February 7, 2025
