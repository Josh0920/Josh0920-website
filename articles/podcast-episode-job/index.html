Podcast Episode — Job

Joshua M. Haub · March 4, 2023

My podcast, “What Does It Say?” has a new episode, Job Chapter 5.

On this podcast, we seek to examine the words of scripture in a straightforward and helpful way.

The book of Job is perhaps one of the book of the Bible many people “overlook.” Perhaps we see it as being “too sad,” or simply confusing. Maybe we would “prefer to study a more ‘uplifting book.’ One that would make me feel good about myself or about my walk with Christ.” Yet the message of Job is one all Christians need! Sometimes, we do suffer. Perhaps we try and mask that reality (in a sense) by NOT studying this book. But, we need to study all the book of the Bible. The message of the book: “Are there people who are willing to be righteous even if it means they will suffer for it?” … “Are there people who are willing to suffer for the sake of righteousness/godliness even if there seems to be no ‘reward’ or relief here on earth?” … “Is being righteous just my ‘vending machine’ to being blessed, or do I see being righteous as something more… something bigger than this life?”

Suffering can be either bring us closer to God, or drive us away. Which path will you choose?

To God be the glory.
