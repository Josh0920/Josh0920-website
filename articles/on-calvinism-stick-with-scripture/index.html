On Calvinism — Stick With Scripture

Joshua M. Haub · August 15, 2025

Recently I have been working through a book explaining Calvinism.

An important reminder when handling Scripture: do not add details to the illustrations the Bible uses.

When Jesus says “born again” in John 3:3, the point is not that we are little babies and “the unbeliever cannot take one step toward his rebirth” (see citation below). THE POINT is that we must start over (kill the Old Man and rise as the New Man).

Also, man-made illustrations DO NOT prove points. I can make up an illustration to make any point I want.

KEY: stick with Scripture.

(Palmer, Edwin H., and Michael Horton. The Five Points of Calvinism: A Study Guide. Baker, 1996)

To God be the Glory.
