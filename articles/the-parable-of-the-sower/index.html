The Parable of the Sower

Joshua M. Haub · November 14, 2025

The Parable of the Sower (Matt 13:1ff) asks a very important question: When I hear God’s word, how do I respond to it? There are four different responses, but only one is the correct one (the one which hears, understands, and bears fruit). This text is often used to talk about how non-believers will respond to evangelism. That is true, but I think it is more than that.

What is my response when I hear God’s word?

When was the last time you heard a challenging, convicting message? Did you change and bear fruit like the good soil, or did you respond like one of the other soils?

The Parable of the Sower challenges us. It is not a “one time experience,” but a daily choice to listen, understand, and bear fruit.

To Him be the Glory.
