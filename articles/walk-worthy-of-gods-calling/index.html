Walk Worthy of God’s Calling

Joshua M. Haub · February 20, 2026

I want to be hungrier for Christ and His word than I am for the meal after services this morning

I want to be more touched by what God has done for me and the worship we give together than I am by posts on social media

I want to be more focused on God in worship than I am focused on everything else but Him

I want to be more changed and recentered every time I worship than I am by “deep quotes” or clips on Instagram and Facebook

I want to be more dedicated to changing for Him than I am vocal about the constant “one day,” “sometime,” “soon,” “eventually”… which almost always yields little to no result

I want to actually give God my best and not just say that I am and then give Him the bare minimum it takes to keep my conscience at peace

I don’t just want to show up and sit in a pew, I want to walk worthy of God’s calling.

To Him Be the Glory.

Joshua M. Haub · February 20, 2026
